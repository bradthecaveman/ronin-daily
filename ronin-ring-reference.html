RONIN

Reference only — no gameplay. Concentric rings of stones; you can always follow a ring around, but you cross inward only where a road (a “stair”) links two rings. The occasional fork gives a route choice. Reach the throne.
rings = the three tiers (outer→inner, lightening toward the throne)  ·  thin roads = orbit a ring freely  ·  warm roads = the sparse inward links (gates/forks)  ·  red = ronin & emperor  ·  dark = guards
