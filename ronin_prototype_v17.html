Rules (v17): 4 tiers (bottom → middle → inner → emperor). Tier changes only via stair tiles. Bottom: 2-row outer ring. Middle: 2-row ring. Inner: 2-row ring. Emperor: single center tile (unwalkable).
Stairs: bottom-tier = corners; middle-tier = N/S/E/W midpoints; inner-tier = midpoints of inner sides (N/S/E/W).
Ronin: moves up to 3 steps (4 if hidden), can leap-capture by moving over an army piece, Grapple (press "g", swap with adjacent enemy, 2 uses), Ascend: when on inner-tier tile adjacent to Emperor click the Emperor tile to rescue (uses the Ronin turn).
Army: pieces = 6 bottom / 4 middle / 2 inner. Army moves up to 2 distinct pieces 1 square each (3 if alerted). Army captures by moving onto Ronin's square.
