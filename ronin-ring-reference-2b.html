RONIN

Tier-gate model. Three tiers of two rings each; move freely inside a tier, cross inward only at a gate. Grey stones mark the gates. Reach the inner tier, then ascend to the throne.
three bands = the tiers (outer→inner, lightening toward the throne)  ·  grey stones = the gates (only crossings inward)  ·  red = ronin & emperor  ·  dark = guards
