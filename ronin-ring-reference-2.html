RONIN

Reference only — the tier-gate model. Three tiers of two rings each. Inside a tier you move freely (orbit and cross between its two rings). Between tiers there’s a wall — cross only at a gate. Reach the inner tier, then ascend to the throne.
three bands = the tiers (outer→inner, lightening toward the throne)  ·  roads = free movement inside a tier  ·  dark rings = tier walls  ·  warm gates = the only crossings inward  ·  red = ronin & emperor  ·  dark = guards
